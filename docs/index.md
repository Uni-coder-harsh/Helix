# Helix: AI Governance Operating System

Welcome to the **Helix Engineering Portal**.

Helix is a secure, transparent, event-driven governance system designed to transform how modern cities manage operations. We bridge the gap between citizen reporting and field operations using spatial intelligence and AI-driven decision support.

## What Problem Does Helix Solve?

Traditional civic platforms are fragmented, slow, and opaque. Citizens report infrastructure issues (e.g. potholes, broken streetlights) into a black hole. Operational officers struggle to prioritize these reports without real spatial context. Politicians lack real-time visibility into the health of their constituencies.

Helix solves this by providing:
1. **Instant Triage:** AI categorizes and prioritizes issues instantly.
2. **Spatial Intelligence:** Map-based visualizations help officers see hotspots (e.g., clusters of water logging).
3. **Proactive Interventions:** Helix drafts decision briefs, suggests interventions, and generates dispatch plans using LLMs.
4. **Full Transparency:** Citizens can track the exact status and timeline of their reports.

## Who Uses Helix?

Helix provides dedicated experiences based on role-based access controls:

### 🏙️ Citizen
Citizens report issues, upvote community problems, and track resolutions. They interact with a simplified dashboard showing the real-time status of their neighborhood.

### 👮 Officer
Officers monitor city operations. They are equipped with the **Spatial Intelligence Map** and **Proactive Morning Briefings** generated by AI. They review AI-drafted "Decision Briefs" and approve dispatch pipelines to fix infrastructure issues.

### 🏛️ MLA & MP
Elected representatives monitor constituency-wide analytics. They use the **Planning** dashboard to approve large-scale projects and monitor real-time approval workflows with budgetary compliance.

### ⚙️ System Administrator
Administrators handle platform integrity, monitoring identity verification, and overseeing RBAC (Role-Based Access Control) policies.

## The Helix Workflow

1. **Intake & Verification**: A Citizen submits a report (e.g., "Pothole on MG Road") via the Next.js frontend.
2. **AI Classification**: The FastAPI backend routes the event to Gemini AI, which classifies the category, severity, and tags.
3. **Spatial Aggregation**: The issue is plotted on MapLibre and clustered with nearby similar reports.
4. **Decision Intelligence**: AI drafts a Decision Brief for the Officer, recommending a specific action (e.g., "Dispatch PWD road repair crew").
5. **Human-in-the-Loop Approval**: The Officer reviews the brief on their dashboard, clicks "Approve", and the issue transitions to "In Progress".
6. **Closing the Loop**: The Citizen's timeline is instantly updated to show that action has been taken.
